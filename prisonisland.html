Prison Island

Length: Half Day

Visit the historic island of Prison Island, named after the prison ruins built in 1893. Take a scenic boat ride from Stone Town, which takes about 30 minutes, to reach this unique destination. The island is now the home of the Aldabra Land Giant Tortoises, which you can observe in their natural habitat. Enjoy a visit to this magical place and discover the island's rich history and beautiful wildlife.

    What you can expect:

  • pickup from your hotel
  • transfer to Stone Town
  • boat transfer to the pristine Prison Island
  • visit of the island
  • see giant land tortoises once brought from the Seychelles
  • boat transfer back to Stone Town
  • transfer back to your hotel

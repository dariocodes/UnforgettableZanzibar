About us

Unforgettable Zanzibar is a premier tour company based in Zanzibar and Tanzania. We specialize in providing an extensive range of wildlife safari experiences and affordable package options for all the best activities on the island.

Our expert guides, who are all local to Zanzibar, will take you to the most popular and picturesque attractions on the island, and our team at our Kianga location will go above and beyond to make your holiday truly unforgettable. With our in-depth knowledge of the island and its culture, we guarantee a truly authentic and unforgettable experience.

Nakupenda

Length: Full Day

Experience the unique beauty of a sandbank in the middle of the ocean! Departing from Stone Town, board a boat and journey for 30 minutes to reach the secluded destination. Once there, set up a tent for protection from the sun and spend the day relaxing, swimming, and indulging in tropical fruits. Enjoy a delicious seafood picnic lunch while taking in the breathtaking views. This is the perfect place to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and soak in the natural beauty of the ocean.

    What you can expect:

  • pickup from your hotel
  • transfer to Stone Town
  • transfer with boat to 'Nakupenda Sandbank'
  • relax time on the sandbank
  • seafood lunch included
  • soft drinks included
  • plate of fruits included
  • transfer with boat back to Stone Town
  • transfer back to your hotel

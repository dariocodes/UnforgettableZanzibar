Cultural/Village Tours

Length: Full Day

Explore the cultural richness of Zanzibar Island on a fascinating village tour. Known for its diverse history shaped by various explorers and colonial rulers, a cultural tour is one of the most interesting and popular activities on Pemba and Unguja Island.

The majority of the island's residents are fishermen, but there are also small farmers. During your tour, you will have the opportunity to visit villages and schools, giving you a firsthand experience of how the people of Zanzibar live in terms of socially, economically and politically.

To fully immerse yourself in the cultural experience, it is recommended to observe the culture of the local people and dress respectfully. Additionally, it is also recommended to bring small gifts such as pens, pencils, or toys for the local community. The tour will take 3-4 hours and it is a unique way to gain a deeper understanding of the island's cultural heritage.

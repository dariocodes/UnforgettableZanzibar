Day Trip Safari to Mikumi

Length: Full Day

Tanzania is one of the most popular East African countries for safari experiences, and for good reason. The country is home to one of the most spectacular natural phenomena in the world, the annual migration of over a million wildebeest (gnu). This incredible event has been captured in countless wildlife documentaries, showcasing the awe-inspiring sight of thundering hooves as the wildebeest make their way from the Serengeti in the south to the Masai Mara in the north.

But safaris in Tanzania are about more than just the famed Big Five (lion, leopard, elephant, rhinoceros, and buffalo). The country is home to thousands of other creatures, both big and small, each with their own unique charm and beauty. From the world's tallest animal, the giraffe, with its elegant and graceful movements, to some of the smallest and most fascinating creatures, such as the colorful and elusive bushbaby, Tanzania's wildlife is truly diverse and captivating.

Unforgettable Zanzibar is a great option for those looking to experience the best of Tanzania's wildlife while also enjoying the island's beautiful beaches. They can arrange day trips to Mikumi National Park, one of the most beautiful parks in Tanzania, and it's affordable price. So whether you're a seasoned safari-goer or a first-time visitor, Tanzania offers an unforgettable experience that will leave you with memories to last a lifetime.

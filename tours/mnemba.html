Mnemba Trip

Length: Half Day

Discover the best marine reserve in the north east of the island, where you can satisfy your snorkeling and diving desires. Witness a wide variety of fish and living corals and possibly even see and swim with dolphins. This marine reserve is a true paradise for ocean enthusiasts, with its rich marine life and stunning underwater views.

    What you can expect:

  • pickup from your hotel
  • transfer with boat to the proximity of the Mnemba Island
  • snorkeling in the beautiful clear waters of the ocean
  • soft drinks & plate of fruits included
  • possibly watch and swim with dolphins in the area
  • transfer back to your hotel

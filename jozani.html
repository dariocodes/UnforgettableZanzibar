Jozani Forest

Length: Half Day

Explore the natural beauty of Jozani-Chwaka Bay National Park and Biosphere Reserve, the largest endogenous forest on the island. Home to the endemic red colobus monkey and other species of monkeys, this park is a must-see for nature lovers. The park is also rich in diverse vegetation, including mangroves, and is a great destination to explore the island's unique flora and fauna.

    What you can expect:

  • pickup from your hotel
  • visit Jozani Forest where you will see Red Columbus monkeys
  • visit of the mangroves
  • optional: Spice Farm tour and Stone Town tour
  • transfer back to your hotel
